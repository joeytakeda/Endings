The Endings Project

Building Sustainable Digital Humanities Projects

How do and how should DH projects conclude?

The Endings Project is a five-year project funded by the Social Sciences and Humanities Research Council (SSHRC) that is creating tools, principles, policies and recommendations for digital scholarship practitioners to create accessible, stable, long-lasting resources in the humanities.

Some of the questions we are attempting to answer include “how should we preserve projects to retain their dynamic features” & “where should projects be archived?” To learn more about us, visit our About Page.
